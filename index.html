What does OTU mean?

Operational Taxonomic Unit.

Operational taxonomic unit or OTU is considered as the basic unit used in numerical taxonomy. These ufer to an individual, species, genus, or class. In numerical methods, the taxonomic units used are constantly not comparable to the formal taxonomic units.

Source and link to the full article: CD Genomics-MicrobioSeq.

How to clean your Belly Button and Prevent Infection?

Dip your finger or a soft washcloth in a solution of saltwater (about a teaspoon of table salt in a cup of warm water) and gently massage the inside of your navel. This should loosen stubborn germs that can cause odor. Then rinse with plain water and pat it dry.

Source and link to the full article: UPMC Health Beat.

Fun Fact

The most common skin bacteria encountered on participants in the Belly Button Biodiversity Project belonged to the groups Staphylococci, Corynebacteria, Actinobacteria, Clostridiales, and Bacilli.
